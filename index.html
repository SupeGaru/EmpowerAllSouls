About Us

What drives us? Why do we partake in social activities?

We are 'Empower All Souls'!

We have the passion, dedication and committment to the noble cause of women empowerment, social upliftment, and enhancing the lives of women and children. At the heart of our mission lies the belief that every woman and child deserves the opportunity to lead a life of dignity, independence, and fulfillment.

Established in 2021, we want to create a positive and lasting change in the lives of women and children. Our focus is on addressing the multifaceted challenges that hinder their progress and potential. Through a variety of impactful initiatives, we strive to break barriers, foster inclusivity, and build a society where every woman and child can thrive.

At the core of our work is the empowerment of women through skill development and entrepreneurship programs. We envision a world where women not only have equal opportunities but also play pivotal roles as leaders, change-makers, and contributors to their communities.

Join us in our journey towards creating a brighter and more equitable future. Together, we can uplift communities, break down barriers, and empower women and children to reach their full potential. Thank you for being a part of the Empower All Souls family.

Ped Lagaye Abhiyaan 2022

Plantation drive to strive for a greener future

In a concerted effort to contribute to environmental sustainability, our NGO recently organized a successful plantation drive. With the overarching goal of combatting deforestation and fostering a greener future, our team, alongside enthusiastic volunteers, planted a multitude of saplings across designated areas. This initiative not only aims to counter the effects of climate change but also fosters community engagement and awareness about the vital role trees play in preserving our planet. Together, we are sowing the seeds for a healthier and more sustainable tomorrow.

  • Date: 19th September 2022
  • Location: Agra, UP

Ayushman Bharat Digital Initiative

Revolutionizing self-sufficiency in healthcare

Under the Ayushman Bharat Initiative started by the Goverment of India, we held a workshop for women where we built AABHA cards digitally for the participants of the workshop. These AABHA cards are a one-stop solution for all the health information and metrics for each individuals.

  • Date: 2nd October 2023
  • Location: Dwarka, Delhi

Mental Health Workshop

Taking a break and taking care of ourselves

Our recent mental health workshop, hosted by our NGO, provided a supportive space for participants to explore and prioritize mental well-being.
